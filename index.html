Who we are

Volunteer solidarity, every single day

For many years, the group has stood beside families facing hardship and has offered practical help with humanity and care.

It receives no state subsidy and relies entirely on volunteers, supporters and people who believe in solidarity.

In numbers

Our impact over the last five years

With the help of volunteers and supporters, Apostoli Agapis has supported people across everyday life, emergencies and humanitarian action.

250 families receive food and clothing support every month
500 children receive school supplies every year
120 people found work through the group's network
350 families were equipped with furniture and appliances
440 families received blankets, bedding and carpets
18 people with disabilities received wheelchairs
